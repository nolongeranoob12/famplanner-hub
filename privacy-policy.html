Privacy Policy

Family Connect Hub
Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Family Connect Hub ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Display name and profile information
  • Phone number (optional, visible only to family members)
  • Photos submitted within the app
  • Family activity posts and shopping list data
  • Device information for push notifications

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's features
  • To sync data in real time across family members
  • To send push notifications about family activities
  • To enable WhatsApp calling between family members

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is only shared with family members you have connected with through the app's invite code system.

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase cloud infrastructure. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time
